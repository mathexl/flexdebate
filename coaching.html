COACHING PACKAGE

Flex Debate’s curriculum is centered around the template model, a unique perspective on debate that any style of debate can be mastered by consistently deploying the same outline for its associated speeches. By emphasizing on repetition, mastery, and contextualization, the template model offers a unique way for students to expand their debate toolbox.

Flex Debate coaching offers all of this at a competitive rate of $3,500 ($1,000 for Sept/Oct, $1,000 for Nov/Dec, and $1,500 for Jan/Feb)

Drills

Flex debate students are guaranteed 12 drills per topic. Drills are tailored to a student’s specific set of skills while introducing new concepts.

Topic Research

Includes receiving a comprehensive card file/util prep produced by our coaches, a topic analysis outlining potential positions on new topics, specific work/edits for cases students want to produce, and specific drills tailored to deploying prep written.

Content Lectures

Flex Debate staff offers students unique content lectures on complex philosophy, critical literature, and strategy employed in debate.

Tournament Coaching

At specific tournaments, students can receive advice concerning particular match-ups, judge adaptations, and judge preffing strategy.
