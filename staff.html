OUR STAFF

Sam Azbel

Founder, Head Coach
Lake Highland Prep ’14
Bowdoin College ’18

As a debater, Sam received 5 bids to the TOC his senior year. Throughout Sam’s time as a coach, his students have accumulated a total of 71 bids to the TOC, and several of his students have made it to elimination rounds at TOC. Sam’s students have also won Valley, The New York City Invitational, Yale Invitational, Florida Blue Key, Scarsdale (twice), Princeton, Strake Jesuit/TDC, Lexington, Basking Ridge, The Valley Sophomore throw down, The Valley Round Robin, and have gotten to finals and semi-finals at many many more.

Muhammad Khattak

Assistant LD Coach
Lake Highland Prep ’18
Stanford University ’22

Muhammad debated for Lake Highland Prep for 5 years on the local on national circuit. He qualified to the Tournament of Champions his sophomore, junior, and senior year, attaining a total of 12 career bids. Some notable achievements of his include championing the Strake Jesuit Invitational/RR, Valley, and semifinals at Bronx and Yale.

Brianna Aaron

Assistant LD Coach
Newark Science High School ‘18
Wake Forest University ‘22

Brianna is currently a debater for Wake Forest University. She attained a total of 11 career bids, 1 in policy and 10 in Lincoln-Douglas. Her most notable achievements were winning the Dukes and Bailey, championing Glenbrooks, and reaching finals of 4 other major national tournaments. As a coach, her students have attained a variety of bids and reached elim rounds of national tournaments, including semis of the TOC, elims of the Barkley Forum, and more.

Reed Weiler

Assistant LD Coach
Lexington High School, MA, ‘18
American University, DC, ‘21

Reed debated LD for four years at Lexington High School. He went to TOC his junior and senior years, receiving 5 bids his senior year and reaching out-rounds of tournaments like Valley, Bronx, Glenbrooks, Emory, and Harvard along the way. He reached finals of the Blake invitational and Harvard Round Robin and championed the Bronx and Harrison Round Robins. As a coach, Reed’s students championed the Lexington, Scarsdale, and Harvard tournaments, accumulating over 6 bids and reaching numerous bid rounds.

Jada Bourne

Administrative Director
Keller HS ’19
Howard University ’23

Jada debated for 3 years at Keller High School and currently debates for Howard University. As a debater, she was a two-time qualifier for TFA state and participated in out rounds her Senior year. She will be joining Flex as an Administrative Director to help with scheduling, organization of the dropbox files, and other administrative tasks to help Flex run smoothly.
