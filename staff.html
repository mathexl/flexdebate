OUR STAFF

Sam Azbel

Founder, Head Coach
Lake Highland Prep ’14
Bowdoin University ’18

As a debater, Sam received 5 bids to the TOC his senior year. Throughout Sam’s time as a coach, his students have accumulated a total of 71 bids to the TOC, and several of his students have made it to elimination rounds at TOC. Sam’s students have have also won Valley, The New York City Invitational, Yale Invitational, Florida Blue Key, Scarsdale (twice), Princeton, Strake Jesuit/TDC, Lexington, Basking Ridge, The Valley Sophomore throw down, The Valley Round Robin, and have gotten to finals and semi-finals at many many more.

Jerome Nashed

Assistant Coach
Lake Highland Prep ’18
Brown University ’22

Jerome debated for five years at Lake Highland Prep, reaching TOC his senior year. He’s joining Flex Debate as an administrative assistant to help organize the growing coaching service. In addition to making sure Flex Debate runs smoothly, he will also be streamlining the dropbox and will act as a file clerk. When you need a certain argument before round, Jerome will be able to locate it and send it to you in order for you to focus on argumentation rather than waste time trying to find a file.
